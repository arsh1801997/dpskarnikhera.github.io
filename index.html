Principal Desk

Dashmesh Public School-KarniKhera is one of the most trusted names in quality education, which is recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence. It was established in 2004 and it is one of the largest chains of PSEB schools in Punjab. It is affiliated to PSEB, Mohali and offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII.

In a very short span of time we have established it as one of the best institutions in Punjab. We have proliferated into a multidisciplinary institution, offering various facilities to impart varied expertise to keep pace with the world. The school offers courses that focus on holistic development of individuals by cultivating their unique talents.
